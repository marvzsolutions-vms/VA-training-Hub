ROLE TESTING
Owner
- Sign in as Owner.
- Badge counts every submitted, under_review, more_information_required, and
  recommended request.
- Approved, declined, and cancelled requests are excluded.

Manager
- Sign in as Manager.
- Badge counts requests at the manager stage: under_review,
  more_information_required, and recommended.
- Submitted requests awaiting coach review are not counted.

Coach
- Sign in as Coach.
- Badge counts only submitted, under_review, and more_information_required
  requests for students visible to that coach under the existing coach/student
  access rules.
- Recommended requests are no longer counted because coach action is complete.

Student
- Sign in as Student.
- No badge appears beside My level.
